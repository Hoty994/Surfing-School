Who we are

VILAMOURA SURF SCHOOL

Algarve South coast Perfect waves for your experience

Was founded in 2006, being the FIRST SURF SCHOOL OF VILAMOURA BEACH, ALGARVE. Our school is certified by the Portuguese Surfing Federation and by Turismo de Portugal. The School aims to teach the art of surfing the waves in a safe, correct and relaxed way, always under the close and attentive supervision of teachers. Thousands of people have already learned from us to slide on the wave wall on top of a board.

We have a team of qualified and experienced instructors, who are passionate about surfing and dedicated to providing the best learning experience for our students. Whether you are a beginner looking to catch your first wave or an experienced surfer aiming to improve your skills, we have the right course for you.

ACTIVITIES

Activities

without rip currents - SANDY BEACHES your best adventure

We offer a variety of activities to suit all levels and interests, including private lessons, group lessons, surf and SUP rentals, stand-up paddleboarding, and surf trips. Our experienced instructors will guide you through the basics of surfing, from paddling and standing up on the board to catching waves and improving your technique.
